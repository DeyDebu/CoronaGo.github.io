About COVID-19

What is COVID-19 (Corona-Virus)

COVID-19 is the infection disease caused by the most recently discovered coronavirus. This new virus and disease were unknown before the outbreak began in Wuhan, China, in December 2019.

Coronaviruses are a large family of viruses which may cause illness in animal or humans.In humans, several Coronaviruses are know to cause respiratory infections rangign from the common cold to more severe diseases such as Middle East Respiratory Syndrom (MERS) and Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrom (SARS). The most recently discovered coronavirus causes coronavirus disease COVID-19.

6 Steps Prevention Against Coronavirus

Wash your hands regularly for 20 seconds, with soap and water or alcohol-based hand senitizer

Cover your nose and mouth with a disposable tissue or flexed elbow when cough or sneeze

Avoid close contact (1 meter or 3 feet) with people who are unwell

Stay home and self-isolate from others in the household if you feel unwell

Stay informed by watching news & follow recommended practices

If you have fever,cough and difficulty breathing, seek medical care early
